Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Kashi Vishwanath Temple located in Varanasi is one of the most sacred temples dedicated to Lord Shiva and is among the twelve Jyotirlingas in India.

Tirupati Balaji Temple

Tirupati Balaji Temple in Andhra Pradesh is one of the richest and most visited pilgrimage destinations in the world.

Somnath Temple

Somnath Temple in Gujarat is an ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva and holds great religious and historical importance.

Kedarnath Temple

Kedarnath Temple situated in the Himalayas of Uttarakhand is a major pilgrimage site and part of the Char Dham Yatra.

Badrinath Temple

Badrinath Temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu is located in Uttarakhand and is visited by thousands of devotees every year.

Siddhivinayak Temple

Siddhivinayak Temple in Mumbai is one of the most famous temples of Lord Ganesha and attracts many devotees daily.

Meenakshi Temple

Meenakshi Temple in Madurai is known for its magnificent architecture and is dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi.

Jagannath Temple

Jagannath Temple located in Puri Odisha is famous for the annual Rath Yatra festival celebrated with great devotion.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple in Amritsar is the holiest shrine of Sikhism and is known for its beautiful golden structure.

Vaishno Devi Temple

Vaishno Devi Temple located in Jammu and Kashmir is one of the most visited pilgrimage destinations in India.
