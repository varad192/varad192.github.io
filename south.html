Festivals of South India

  • Pongal: A harvest festival celebrated in Tamil Nadu where people prepare a special dish called Pongal and offer it to the Sun God.
  • Onam: A major festival in Kerala that marks the homecoming of King Mahabali. It includes boat races, traditional dances, and grand feasts.
  • Ugadi: New Year festival celebrated in Andhra Pradesh and Karnataka with joy and rituals.
  • Vishu: Celebrated in Kerala with prayers, lights, and special food.
