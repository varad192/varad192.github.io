Introduction

India is one of the oldest civilizations in the world with a history of more than 5000 years. It is famous for its rich culture, traditions, festivals, languages, and heritage. India is a democratic country where people of different religions and cultures live together peacefully.

The country is known worldwide for Yoga, Ayurveda, Bollywood, classical music, dance forms and historical monuments.

Quick Facts About India

Capital New Delhi
National Animal Tiger
National Bird Peacock
National Flower Lotus
Currency Indian Rupee (₹)
National Language Hindi

Why India is a Tourist Paradise?

India offers snow-covered Himalayas, golden deserts of Rajasthan, peaceful beaches of Goa, backwaters of Kerala, dense forests, wildlife sanctuaries, historical monuments, and spiritual destinations.

Every state has its own unique culture, food, dress and traditions.
