About North India
North India is known for the Himalayas, historical monuments, holy rivers and royal culture. It is home to famous tourist attractions like the Taj Mahal and beautiful hill stations.

Popular Tourist Places
Place State Famous For
Agra Uttar Pradesh Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, Fatehpur Sikri.
Jaipur Rajasthan Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace.
Manali Himachal Pradesh Popular for snow, skiing, and honeymooners.
Delhi Delhi Capital city, India Gate, Red Fort, Humayun’s Tomb.
Srinagar Jammu & Kashmir Dal Lake, houseboats, Mughal Gardens.
Amritsar Punjab Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh.

Special Attractions
• Snow-covered Himalayas
• Historical monuments
